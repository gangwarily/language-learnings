## üéØ WORD ORDER SUMMARY

### **Three Different Patterns:**

---

### **Pattern 1: CONNECTIVE ADVERBS**


**Example:**
"Ich war m√ºde. **[Deshalb] [bin] [ich]** fr√ºh ins Bett gegangen."
