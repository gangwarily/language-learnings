## üé§ Speaking Practice Integration

### Daily Voice Journaling
**Process:**
1. Get a speaking prompt (from weekly check-in or self-selected)
2. Think about it briefly (don't script it!)
3. Record yourself answering for 5-10 minutes
4. Note where you got stuck or weren't sure
5. Continue - don't stop to look things up

**Weekly Review:**
1. Identify your top 3-5 struggles from the week
2. Share them in check-in
3. Get correct versions and explanations

## üìù Grammar Reference

### Case System Quick Reference

**Always Accusative:** bis, durch, f√ºr, gegen, ohne, um
**Always Dative:** aus, au√üer, bei, mit, nach, seit, von, zu
**Always Genitive:** (an)statt, trotz, w√§hrend, wegen
**Two-Way (Acc/Dat):** an, auf, hinter, in, neben, √ºber, unter, vor, zwischen
- Accusative = movement/destination (Wohin?)
- Dative = location (Wo?)

### Adjective Ending Patterns

**With Definite Article (der/die/das):**
- Nominative: -e
- All others: -en

**With Indefinite Article (ein/eine):**
- Mixed endings (changes based on case/gender)

**No Article:**
- Strong endings (adjective takes over article's job)
