## Tips Before You Start

**Remember:**
- Two-way prepositions: movement (accusative) vs. location (dative)
- Common prepositions: mit, nach, von, zu = dative
- f√ºr, ohne, durch, gegen = accusative
- w√§hrend, trotz, wegen = genitive (the tricky ones!)
- Modal verbs send the infinitive to the end
- Subordinate clauses (weil, dass) send the verb to the end

**Don't stress about:**
- Getting everything perfect (you won't, and that's okay!)
- Looking things up if you're completely stuck (but try first!)
- Small spelling mistakes (focus on structure and cases)

**Do focus on:**
- Identifying what case each noun needs to be in
- Matching adjective endings to article + case + gender
- Verb placement (especially with modals and subordinate clauses)
